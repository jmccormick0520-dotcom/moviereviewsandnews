The Fantastic Four: First Steps (2025) 4K Blu-ray review

The Fantastic Four: First Steps is our latest MCU movie to hit the 4K format, and I think it is... FANTASTIC! Matt Shakman has delievered us the perfect Fantastic Four movie, with all of the heart and optimism and thrills that Marvel's First Family are known for. Pedro Pascal is the perfect Reed Richards, showing us his intellegence as well as his vulnerabilities over fatherhood and his guilt regarding the accident in space. Vanessa Kirby and Joseph Quinn and great as the Storm siblings, and Ebon Moss-Bachrach brings the film a soul and beating heart as Ben Grimm aka The Thing. The film is loaded with action and superhero thrills. I think this movie shows us that Matt Shakman could make a perfect Star Trek movie. Maybe one day. This is one of the finest MCU movies and a must watch before Avengers: Doomsday this December.

Techincal Review

The Fantastic Four arrive on 4K Blu-ray from Walt Disney Studios and Sony Pictures Home Entertainment. The picture quality is great but not mind-blowing. The film has a unique retro-futuristic style to it and the 4K visuals and high bitrate enhance the image. The HDR is present but subtle. Johnny's fire effects burn brighter and everything looks a bit more refined. The film is presented with a Dolby Atmos audio track and it is a banger. Micheal Giacchino's score envelops you in awesome Fantastic Four action. The height channels are used sparingly yet effectively. This is another winner from Marvel Studios.

The Verdict

The Fantastic Four: First Steps is a fantastic movie with a fantastic home video presentation. Marvel has delivered a great film loaded with action and heart. I can't recommend it enough. 5 out of 5 stars.
